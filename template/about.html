    We are Fresh water fish farmers


    "MARICULTURE - A web application for fish farmers for selling their fishes to consumers directly. Now a days the lifestyle of the people is different. People feel uncomfortable and time consume for going crowded markets. So, e-shopping is a boon as it saves lot of time. Also, now a days we all are got the fishes that are preserved in some chemicals, and that chemicals is typically injected into the fishes.

    So, by this website we can eat fresh fishes also. MARICULTURE enables the farmers to sell their fishes and buy fish feeds directly without an intermediary service over the internet. Through this website, the consumers can find out the details of the fishes and the fish feeds and can place their required orders. Also, it connect to the lessors who upload about the farming ponds, and the details of the ponds. By this application those who are interested to do fish farming are find out a good land (pond) and setup for making fish farming."
